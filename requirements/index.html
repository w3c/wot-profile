Profile Registry

  1. The specification shall define a registry [[w3c-process]] of Profiles to which WoT Things and WoT Consumers may conform, including:
    1. A registry definition which defines the fields of the registry table, the policy for modifying entries, the custodian of the registry, and what an individual Profile specification may contain
    2. A registry table which enumerates the registered Profiles
  2. The registry definition shall guide the custodian to aim to keep the number of registered profiles as small as possible, by rejecting registrations for profiles that significantly overlap in technologies or use cases, in order to minimise fragmentation on the Web of Things
